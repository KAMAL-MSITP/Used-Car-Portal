Answers to common questions

How can I quickly get my car valued?

Just fill out the online form, and one of our consultants will send you an indicative offer via email or SMS.

When and how will I be paid?

Before handover, all payments are made electronically.

What is the duration of the process?

Usually, it takes us fifteen minutes to finish the inspection, the paperwork, and the payment.

Do you visit me?

Yes, we will set up a time and location that work for you.

Which documents do I need?

Both your driver's licence and the vehicle registration certificate are required. In the event that these are misplaced or lost, please contact 1300 926 833 to discuss your options.

Can I sell without a registration or roadworthy certificate?

Indeed, we are permitted to purchase your vehicle without a roadworthy certificate or registration since we are licenced motor dealers.

Will you purchase a financed car?

Of course! The first thing to do is get in touch with your financier and ask for a written request for the loan payout amount. We give you any remaining funds after paying the financier the entire outstanding balance. In the event that the payout exceeds your valuation, you will be responsible for the difference.
